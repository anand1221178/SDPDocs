Application Outline

Scope

The Campus Safety Application will enhance campus security and safety by providing easy-to-use tools for a variety of services, such as reporting incidents, receiving emergency alerts, and access to safety information.

Key features of the application

Emergency AlertsSend real-time emergency alerts to users about safety threats or
emergencies
Incident ReportingEnable users to report safety incidents directly through the app,
including photos and descriptions.
Safety Resources:Provide access to safety resources, including emergency contacts,
safety tips, and campus safety policies.
Real-Time NotificationsSend notifications for emergency alerts, incident updates,
and safety announcements.
Location Services:Utilise GPS to provide location-specific alerts and allow users to
share their location in case of emergencies.
Safety ridesCall a ride on campus to get taken to your res late at late

The application will not be used to notify organisations outside of the University of the Witwatersrand. It is solely a Wits-only application and will link and integrate with other components of the Wits Smart Campus system.

Users

The system will be important for 2 main users:

Student/StaffA student and/or staff member will use the app to:
1. Receive emergency alerts
2. Report an incident of a specific type
3. Access safety information
4. Use location services
Safety Administrator (could possibly be added)A safety administrator could use the app to:
1. Report an important emergency incident that will notify all app users
2. View reported incidents from students/staff
3. Be notified of emergency events form students/staff for the sake of deploying emergency services

Key reporting types

◦ Medical emergency
◦ Weather issue
◦ Fire incident
◦ Natural disaster
◦ Security threat
